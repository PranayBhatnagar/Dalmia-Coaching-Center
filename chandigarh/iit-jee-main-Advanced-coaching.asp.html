Teaching Methodology

Click Here

 

At ALLEN Chandigarh, each student is special and we aspire to ensure victory of each individual. ALLEN is the leading coaching for IIT-JEE (Main + Advanced) preparation in Chandigarh, which prepare aspirants methodically and analytically to get the most excellent output. The team of experienced and stable faculty members at Chandigarh backed by strong academic and administrative system ensures students learning of basics to the grass root level during concept building sessions.

Smartly crafted JEE (Main+Advanced) Division strongly molds and crafts the aspirants with what they desire to be, where they aspire to go and how they want to make it. JEE (Main + Advanced) preparation requires a sharp, inquisitive and dedicated mind, focused attention, constant efforts and severe discipline. Targeting an Engineering career with the solid foundations at ALLEN Chandigarh center is gainful for the students and provides them an edge.

Problem solving skills are developed via guided self-practice using comprehensive study material. Self-confidence, time management & scoring aptitude are built through regular Tests and Smart Feedback System. In addition to this, one-to-one interaction, personal care, assistance and motivational surroundings lead aspirants to accomplish the best ranks in IIT-JEE and other Engineering Entrance Examinations.

Our teaching methodology consists of concept building classes, smart study material and intelligent test system. To get the best all-round performance, students are prepared in 3 stages - for Boards, JEE MAIN and JEE ADVANCED, aided with doubt removal session.

The phenomenon of ALLEN IIT-JEE coaching classes has already drawn lots of consideration of IIT aspirants all over the country. The ALLEN classroom students of JEE (Main+Advanced) division has proved their excellence by producing best results in IIT-JEE 2015 , which proves the worth and effectiveness of the ALLEN encouraging system and competitive environment with a large pool of talented students and faculties.

Utmost care is taken in the preparation of test papers in an attempt to assure greater consistency with the real test papers.
